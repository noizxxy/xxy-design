Graphic design communicates with the public through color, composition, text, and visual stimuli, and its purpose is to communicate with the public through visual elements and convey information. Of course, in addition to serving any group or solving a product problem, design can also be used to express personal ideas to a certain extent, and visuals are an amazing perception that can break down any barriers to reach a commercial or conceptual communication.

As a designer who loves beautiful things, I love strong visual effects and the beautiful experience that good looking things bring to people. In the process of creating works, I hope to solve some problems through a good visual experience, and to deliver some better understood letters in a more eye-catching form, I am happy to try any form of art, graphic design is just a way of information exchange, I will incorporate more experimental design and some whimsical ideas to make the works more interesting and artistic. At the same time, I also love to create and publish some personal publications, these works contain some of my ideas and attitudes about the world, you are also welcome to communicate with me！
